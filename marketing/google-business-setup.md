# Google Business Profile Setup — Restored by Randi

Step-by-step guide to creating and optimizing your Google Business Profile.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Profile

1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com)
2. Sign in with the Google account you want to manage the business
3. Click **"Add your business to Google"**

---

## Step 2: Enter Business Information

### Business Name
```
Restored by Randi
```

### Business Category
- **Primary:** "Lingerie Store" or "Prosthetics" (search for "breast prosthesis" — pick closest)
- **Additional categories:**
  - Medical Supply Store
  - Swimwear Store
  - Women's Clothing Store

### Business Type
- Select: **"Online retail"** (if no physical storefront)
- OR **"Local store"** if you have a physical location

### Address
- **If you have a storefront:** Enter full address
- **If home-based/online only:** Choose "I deliver goods and services to my customers" and hide your address. Set service area to "United States"

### Service Area
```
United States
```

### Phone Number
```
[Your business phone number]
```
> Tip: Get a dedicated business number (Google Voice is free)

### Website
```
https://restoredbyrandi.com
```

---

## Step 3: Verify Your Business

- **Postcard verification:** Google mails a postcard with a code (5–14 days)
- **Phone verification:** Instant, if available for your category
- **Email verification:** Check if offered
- **Video verification:** Record a video of your business/products if prompted

## Step 4: Optimize Your Profile

```
Restored by Randi is an online store for mastectomy bras, breast prostheses, swimwear, and post-surgery products. Founded by a breast cancer survivor, we offer top brands like Amoena and American Breast Care with personal guidance and compassionate service. Most insurance plans cover our products under federal law (WHCRA). Free shipping on orders over $75. We believe every woman deserves to feel beautiful and confident after mastectomy.
```

### Business Hours
```
Monday–Friday: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM (your time zone)
Saturday: 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM
Sunday: Closed
```

## Step 5: Add Photos

Upload at minimum:

- [ ] **Logo** (square, 250×250px minimum)
- [ ] **Cover photo** (landscape, 1080×608px)
- [ ] **3–5 product photos** (well-lit, on white or lifestyle)
- [ ] **1–2 lifestyle photos** (model wearing products, if available)
- [ ] **Photo of you (the founder)** — builds trust and personal connection

> Add new photos monthly. Businesses with photos get 42% more direction requests and 35% more click-throughs.

## Step 6: Set Up Messaging

1. Go to **Messages** in your GBP dashboard
2. Turn on messaging
3. Set up auto-reply:
```
Thank you for reaching out to Restored by Randi! We typically respond within a few hours during business hours (Mon–Fri 9–5). For immediate help, visit restoredbyrandi.com or call [phone number]. 💗
```

## Step 8: Get Reviews

Reviews are critical for trust and rankings.

### Ask for Reviews
After each order, send a follow-up email:
```
Subject: How was your experience with Restored by Randi?

Hi [Name],

Thank you for your recent order! We hope you love your new [product].

If you have a moment, would you mind leaving us a review on Google? It helps other women find us and means the world to our small business.

[Direct review link]

With gratitude,
Randi
Restored by Randi
```

### Get Your Direct Review Link
1. Go to your GBP dashboard
2. Click **"Ask for reviews"**
3. Copy the short link
4. Use this link in all review requests

### Review Response Templates

**Positive review:**
```
Thank you so much, [Name]! It means everything to know we could help. Wishing you all the best on your journey. 💗 — Randi
```

**Negative review:**
```
[Name], I'm sorry to hear about your experience. We take this seriously and want to make it right. Please reach out to us at [email] so we can help. — Randi
```

## Key Metrics to Track (GBP Insights)

- **Search queries** — what people search to find you
- **Views** — how many people see your profile
- **Actions** — website clicks, calls, direction requests
- **Photo views** — engagement with your images
- **Review count and rating** — aim for 4.5+ stars
