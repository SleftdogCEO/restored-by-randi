# Social Media Plan — Restored by Randi (E-Commerce)

## Platforms
- **Facebook** (primary) — Target demographic is 45–70 women; Facebook is #1
- **Instagram** (secondary) — Visual product showcases, younger survivors
- **Pinterest** (growth) — High search intent for products; evergreen content
- **YouTube** (growth) — Sizing tutorials, product reviews, Randi's story

## Posting Schedule
- **4–5 posts/week** across Facebook + Instagram
- **2 pins/week** on Pinterest
- **1 video/month** on YouTube (minimum)
- Use Meta Business Suite to schedule in batches

## E-Commerce Specific Tactics

### Drive Traffic to Shop
- Every product post includes direct link to shop
- Stories/Reels with "swipe up to shop" or "link in bio"
- Pin top-selling products to top of Facebook/Instagram profiles

### User-Generated Content
- Encourage customers to share photos with hashtag #RestoredByRandi
- Repost customer photos (with permission) — social proof drives sales
- Run monthly "share your story" feature

### Promotions & Sales
- Monthly promo code shared on social (drives urgency)
- Flash sales via Stories (24-hour only)
- "Free shipping Friday" — free shipping on all orders, not just $100+
- Bundle deals promoted via carousel posts

### Pinterest Strategy
- Create boards: "Mastectomy Bras," "Mastectomy Swimwear," "Recovery Tips," "Sizing Guide"
- Pin every product with link to shop
- Pin blog posts and guides
- Rich Pins enabled for products
