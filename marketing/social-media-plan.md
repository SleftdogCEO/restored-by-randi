# Social Media Plan — Restored by Randi

## Platforms
- **Facebook** (primary) — Target demographic is 45–70 women; Facebook is #1
- **Instagram** (secondary) — Visual product showcases, younger survivors

## Posting Schedule
- **3–4 posts/week** across both platforms
- Use Meta Business Suite to schedule in batches (1 month at a time)

---

## Weekly Content Framework

| Day | Content Type | Example |
|-----|-------------|---------|
| Monday | Educational / Insurance tip | "Did you know Medicare covers 6 mastectomy bras per year?" |
| Wednesday | Product spotlight | Photo of new Amoena swimwear collection |
| Friday | Testimonial / Story | Client quote (with permission) or inspirational message |
| Saturday (optional) | Community / Lifestyle | Local event, support group announcement, self-care tip |

---

## Monthly Themes

| Month | Theme | Content Ideas |
|-------|-------|--------------|
| January | New Year, New Confidence | Resolution-style posts, "Treat yourself to a fitting" |
| February | Love Yourself | Self-care, Valentine's self-love |
| March | Spring Refresh | New swimwear arrivals, spring bra styles |
| April | Insurance Education | "How to use your insurance benefits" series |
| May | Mother's Day | Celebrate strength, gift ideas |
| June | Summer Ready | Swimwear showcase, beach confidence |
| July | Mid-Year Check | "Have you used your insurance benefits?" |
| August | Back to Routine | Fall wardrobe, new bra fittings |
| September | Support Month | Support group promotions |
| October | Breast Cancer Awareness | Daily content, pink, partnerships, events |
| November | Gratitude | Client appreciation, thankful posts |
| December | Year-End Benefits | "Use your insurance before it resets!" |

---

## Content Pillars

1. **Education** (30%) — Insurance tips, product care, what to expect
2. **Products** (25%) — New arrivals, product features, styling
3. **Testimonials** (20%) — Client stories, before/after confidence
4. **Community** (15%) — Events, support groups, local partnerships
5. **Behind-the-scenes** (10%) — Randi's story, fitting room, day in the life
