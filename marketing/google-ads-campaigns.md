## Campaign 4: Display/Remarketing

**Objective:** Re-engage website visitors who didn't purchase  
**Daily Budget:** $5/day  
**Bid Strategy:** Target CPA or Maximize conversions  
**Audience:** All website visitors (last 30 days) minus purchasers  
**Network:** Google Display Network  
**Frequency Cap:** 3 impressions per user per day

### Setup Requirements

1. Install Google Ads remarketing tag on all pages
2. Create audience: "All visitors — last 30 days"
3. Create audience: "Purchasers" (thank you page visitors)
4. Target: All visitors MINUS purchasers

### Display Ad Copy (3 Variations)

**Variation 1 — Reminder**
- **Headline:** Still Thinking It Over?
- **Description:** The perfect mastectomy bra is waiting for you at Restored by Randi. Come back and find your fit — your insurance may even cover it.
- **CTA Button:** Shop Now

**Variation 2 — Social Proof**
- **Headline:** Women Love Restored by Randi
- **Description:** Join hundreds of women who've found comfort and confidence after mastectomy. Top brands, personal service, discreet shipping.
- **CTA Button:** See What's New

**Variation 3 — Urgency/Benefit**
- **Headline:** Your Insurance Benefits May Expire
- **Description:** Most plans reset annually. Don't miss out on covered mastectomy bras and breast forms. Shop now at Restored by Randi.
- **CTA Button:** Check Your Coverage

### Display Ad Sizes to Create

- 300×250 (medium rectangle)
- 728×90 (leaderboard)
- 160×600 (wide skyscraper)
- 320×50 (mobile banner)
- Responsive display ad (upload assets, Google auto-sizes)

## Campaign 5: Facebook/Instagram Ads

**Objective:** Build awareness and drive conversions  
**Daily Budget:** $10/day  
**Platform:** Facebook + Instagram (automatic placements)  
**Target Audience:**
- Women 35–75, United States
- Interests: breast cancer awareness, mastectomy, cancer survivor, breast cancer support, Susan G. Komen, American Cancer Society
- Lookalike audience (after 100+ customers): 1% lookalike of purchasers

### Ad 1 — Brand Story (Awareness)

**Format:** Video or carousel  
**Primary Text:**  
"After my mastectomy, I couldn't find a store that felt like it was made for me. So I built one. Restored by Randi is an online shop for mastectomy bras, breast forms, and swimwear — created by a survivor who understands your journey. 💗"  
**Headline:** Founded by a Survivor. Built for You.  
**CTA:** Learn More  
**Link:** About page

### Ad 2 — Blog/Education (Awareness)

**Format:** Single image (lifestyle photo)  
**Primary Text:**  
"Did you know your insurance probably covers mastectomy bras and breast prostheses? Federal law (WHCRA) requires it for most plans. Read our guide to understanding your benefits — and stop paying out of pocket. 📋"  
**Headline:** Your Insurance Rights After Mastectomy  
**CTA:** Learn More  
**Link:** Insurance info blog post

### Ad 3 — Product Showcase (Conversion)

**Format:** Carousel (4–6 products)  
**Primary Text:**  
"Comfortable, beautiful mastectomy bras and breast forms — shipped right to your door. We carry Amoena, ABC, and more. Free shipping on orders over $75. Your insurance may cover your purchase! 🛍️"  
**Headline:** Shop Mastectomy Bras & Breast Forms  
**CTA:** Shop Now  
**Link:** Product collection page

### Ad 4 — Testimonial/Social Proof (Conversion)

**Format:** Single image with quote overlay  
**Primary Text:**  
"'I finally found a store that gets it. The bras are comfortable, the service is personal, and I felt like a real person — not just a patient.' — Shop the products women are loving at Restored by Randi."  
**Headline:** Real Women. Real Comfort. Real Confidence.  
**CTA:** Shop Now  
**Link:** Best sellers page

### Ad 5 — Seasonal/Urgency (Conversion)

**Format:** Single image  
**Primary Text:**  
"New year, new benefits. If your insurance plan just reset, now is the perfect time to get fitted for new mastectomy bras and breast forms. Most plans cover them — we can help you find out. Don't leave your benefits on the table! ⏰"  
**Headline:** Your Insurance Benefits Just Reset  
**CTA:** Shop Now  
**Link:** Homepage or insurance page

### Facebook Ad Tips

- **Creative:** Use warm, real photos (not stock). Show diverse women. Avoid clinical imagery.
- **Testing:** Run ads 1 & 2 for awareness (1 week), then launch 3–5 for retargeting warm audiences.
- **Pixel:** Install Meta Pixel on site for conversion tracking and audience building.
- **Budget Split:** 40% awareness, 60% conversion once retargeting audiences are built.

### Scaling Plan

1. **Month 1:** Start all campaigns at minimum budgets. Focus on data collection.
2. **Month 2:** Kill underperforming keywords/ads. Scale winners by 20%.
3. **Month 3:** Add lookalike audiences on Facebook. Test Shopping ads on Google.
4. **Ongoing:** Review weekly. Adjust bids, budgets, and creative monthly.
