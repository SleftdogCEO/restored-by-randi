# Launch Day Checklist — Restored by Randi

---

## Pre-Launch (Day Before)

- [ ] Final website QA: test every link, form, checkout flow, and page on mobile + desktop
- [ ] Test a real purchase (use a test card or buy and refund)
- [ ] Confirm shipping settings and rates
- [ ] Confirm payment processing is live
- [ ] Confirm email notifications work (order confirm, shipping, etc.)
- [ ] Stage all social media posts (draft or schedule)
- [ ] Draft personal email announcement
- [ ] Prepare Facebook group post templates
- [ ] Take a deep breath — you built something amazing 💗

---

## Launch Day — Hour by Hour

### 6:00 AM — Go Live Checks

- [ ] Verify site is live and accessible
- [ ] Place one more test order to confirm checkout
- [ ] Check that Google Analytics and Meta Pixel are firing
- [ ] Enable Google Ads campaigns (set to "Enabled" — paused until now)
- [ ] Enable Facebook/Instagram ad campaigns

### 7:00 AM — Submit to Search Engines

- [ ] Log into [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
- [ ] Submit sitemap: `https://restoredbyrandi.com/sitemap.xml`
- [ ] Request indexing for homepage
- [ ] Request indexing for top 5 product/collection pages
- [ ] Submit site to Bing Webmaster Tools (free traffic)
