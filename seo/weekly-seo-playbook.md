# Weekly SEO Playbook

## Monday
- Review GSC performance for top queries and pages
- Check index coverage and fix crawl issues
- Refresh internal links from top posts to money pages

## Tuesday
- Update 2 existing posts with new FAQs and internal links
- Improve title/meta CTR on underperforming URLs

## Wednesday
- Publish 1 new high-intent post
- Add 3 contextual links to money pages and local page

## Thursday
- Review competitor SERP snippets for target keywords
- Update FAQ schema and expand featured snippet opportunities

## Friday
- Validate sitemap freshness and robots.txt
- Submit priority URLs in GSC and Bing URL inspection tools
- Track conversions from money pages and quiz starts
