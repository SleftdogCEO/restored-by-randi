Yes! Under the Women's Health and Cancer Rights Act (WHCRA), most insurance plans must cover breast prostheses, mastectomy bras, and related products. Medicare also covers one prosthesis per side every 1-2 years. We handle the paperwork for you.

Most women are ready for a permanent prosthesis 6-8 weeks after surgery, once swelling has gone down and incisions have healed. We can provide a lightweight temporary form much sooner for comfort during your initial recovery.

Your private fitting takes about 60-90 minutes. Randi will listen to your needs, take gentle measurements, and help you try different shapes, sizes, and styles. There's no rush—you'll leave with products that feel natural and look beautiful.

Absolutely. We offer private in-home fittings throughout the greater West Palm Beach area. Many women prefer the comfort and convenience of being fitted at home, especially during early recovery.

Silicone prostheses typically last 1-2 years with proper care. Insurance usually covers replacement every 1-2 years. If your form shows signs of wear, changes in weight or body shape may also warrant a new fitting.

Yes! Silicone prostheses are waterproof. Paired with a mastectomy swimsuit that has a built-in pocket, you can swim, do water aerobics, and enjoy the beach with confidence. We carry a lovely selection of mastectomy swimwear.

We help women after all types of breast surgery. We carry partial shapers and equalizers that restore symmetry beautifully after lumpectomy. You deserve to feel balanced and confident no matter what surgery you've had.
