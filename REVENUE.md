## 2. Insurance Reimbursement — Customer Guidance (Not Direct Billing)

### Important: Online-only businesses generally cannot bill insurance directly.
Direct insurance billing requires in-person fitting in most cases. Instead, we:

1. **Provide itemized receipts** with HCPCS codes (L8030, L8020, A4280)
2. **Include a reimbursement guide** with every order
3. **Help customers submit out-of-network claims** to their insurance
4. **Provide template claim letters** customers can use

### How This Helps Revenue
- Customers are more willing to buy at full retail price knowing they can get reimbursed
- Reduces price sensitivity
- Differentiator from Amazon/generic sellers
- Builds loyalty and trust

### Key Insurance Facts for Customers
- **WHCRA** requires most group health plans to cover mastectomy products
- **Medicare** covers prostheses + bras with a prescription (typically requires in-person, but patients can try submitting with our documentation)
- **Out-of-network reimbursement** typically 60-80% of allowable amount
- **Typical customer reimbursement:** $200–$600 per claim

## 8. Expense Analysis (E-Commerce Model)

### Fixed Monthly Costs
| Expense | Monthly Cost |
|---------|-------------|
| ~~Rent (retail space)~~ | ~~$1,500–$3,000~~ **$0** |
| Home office / workspace | $0 (existing space) |
| Insurance (business + liability) | $150–$300 |
| Software (Shopify/Stripe, Mailchimp, etc.) | $100–$300 |
| Phone/Internet | $100–$150 |
| Shipping supplies (boxes, tape, tissue) | $100–$300 |
| **Total Fixed** | **$450–$1,050** |

### Variable Costs (per sale)
| Expense | % of Revenue |
|---------|-------------|
| Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) | 35–45% |
| Payment processing (Stripe 2.9% + 30¢) | 3–4% |
| Shipping costs (USPS Priority) | 5–10% |
| Packaging materials | 1–2% |
| Marketing/advertising | 8–12% |
| Returns/exchanges processing | 2–3% |
| **Total Variable** | **54–76%** |

### Shipping Cost Breakdown
| Order Value | Shipping Cost | Who Pays |
|-------------|--------------|----------|
| Under $100 | $6–$12 | Customer pays flat $7.95 |
| $100–$200 | $8–$15 | Free (absorbed by business) |
| $200+ | $10–$18 | Free (absorbed by business) |

**Average shipping cost per order: $8–$12**
**Monthly shipping budget (100 orders): $800–$1,200**

### Break-Even Analysis
| Scenario | Monthly Fixed | Contribution Margin | Break-Even Revenue |
|----------|-------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Conservative | $1,050 | 24% (1 - 76%) | **$4,375/month** |
| Moderate | $750 | 36% (1 - 64%) | **$2,083/month** |
| Optimistic | $450 | 46% (1 - 54%) | **$978/month** |

### Break-Even Timeline
- **Conservative:** Month 2–3
- **Moderate:** Month 1–2
- **Optimistic:** Month 1

### Key Advantage: No Rent
Eliminating retail space ($1,500–$3,000/month) dramatically lowers the break-even point compared to a brick-and-mortar model. This savings alone adds $18,000–$36,000/year to the bottom line.

## Revenue Optimization Strategies

### Quick Wins (Month 1-3)
1. **Launch with virtual consultations** — convert browsers to buyers
2. **Bundle products** — prosthesis + 3 bras + accessories = discount
3. **Capture emails** — every visitor joins email list for reorder reminders
4. **Insurance reimbursement guide** — removes price hesitation
5. **Google Shopping ads** — product listings for high-intent searches

### Growth Plays (Month 3-12)
1. **Content marketing** — blog + social + YouTube driving organic traffic
2. **Referral program** — $25 credit for each referred customer
3. **Expand product line** — lymphedema, compression, post-surgical
4. **Amazon marketplace** — list products on Amazon for wider reach
5. **Reorder automation** — email sequences drive repeat purchases

### Scale Plays (Year 2-3)
1. **Private label products** — higher margins on own-brand bras
2. **3PL fulfillment** — outsource shipping as volume grows
3. **Subscription box** — quarterly bra/accessory refresh box
4. **Training/certification program** — train new fitters ($500-$1,000/student)
5. **Wholesale partnerships** — supply cancer centers directly

*This e-commerce revenue model eliminates the overhead of a physical location while serving customers nationwide. The combination of virtual consultations, detailed sizing guides, and insurance reimbursement guidance creates a differentiated online experience that builds trust and loyalty.*
